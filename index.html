Medical Resources Sharing System

About The Website

Medical Resources Sharing System is a dynamic website created to support hospitals and healthcare centers during emergency situations by providing quick access to important medical resources. Users can sign up, log in, and request items such as blood pouches, oxygen cylinders, ICU beds, ventilators, medicines, and other supplies. They can enter the required quantity and hospital location, making the process faster, organized, and efficient.
